Welcome to Chemistry Js

What is Chemistry Js

Chemistry Js is a web app made to help students understand and calculate chemistry concepts.

What can Chemistry Js do

As of the latest beta (beta1), Chemistry Js can solve equilibrim related problems using both initial concentrations and final concentrations. In the next version, Chemistry Js will be able to analize simple organic compounds and display electron configuraion diagrams.
